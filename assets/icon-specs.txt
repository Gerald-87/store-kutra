KUTRA App Icon Specifications
==============================

Design Elements:
- Background: Circular gradient from #8B4513 to #CD853F (brown gradient)
- Size: 1024x1024px for high resolution
- Store Icon: White storefront symbol with brown details at top center
- Text: "KUTRA" in white, bold, sans-serif font (120px)
- Tagline: "Trade meets AI" in smaller white text (32px) below main text
- Style: Modern, clean, professional

Colors:
- Primary: #8B4513 (Saddle Brown)
- Secondary: #A0522D (Sienna)
- Accent: #CD853F (Peru)
- Text: #FFFFFF (White)
- Highlight: rgba(255,255,255,0.9)

Icon Sizes Needed:
- 1024x1024 (App Store)
- 512x512 (Google Play)
- 180x180 (iOS)
- 192x192 (Android)
- 128x128 (Android)
- 96x96 (Android)
- 72x72 (Android)
- 48x48 (Android)
- 36x36 (Android)

Usage:
This icon will appear on users' home screens when they install KUTRA from the app store. The design reflects the marketplace/trading nature of the app with professional branding.
